About the project

~

Description


"Derelict - Survival Game" is an immersive 3D survival experience where players find themselves stranded on a mysterious island. The game unfolds as a roleplaying adventure, combining real-life lessons with engaging tasks and encounters. From mastering camping skills to navigating human nature, Derelict offers more than just a gameâ€”it's a captivating exploration of survival instincts.


Game Features


Embark on a roleplaying journey on a mysterious island, mastering movement, running, climbing, and a variety of weapons. The game introduces a boat driving feature for exploration and presents an engaging storyline filled with tasks and encounters. Derelict is designed to impart lessons on camping skills, human nature, and problem-solving abilities.


Game Mechanics


Premise: The player is stranded alone on an island and must explore to find a way to escape, engaging in various activities and overcoming challenges.

Rules of Progression: Each level has different winning conditions, including escaping, shooting, and interacting with other characters. The game unfolds with progressively challenging sublevels, requiring strategic thinking and precision.

Controls: Navigate with WASD keys, jump with the Spacebar, crouch using [Specify key/button], and shoot with mouse clicks.


System Requirements


Derelict requires a PC for gameplay, featuring keyboard and mouse controls. Arrow keys simulate player movement, and mouse clicks are used for shooting. Additional gameplay elements are yet to be added.


Target Audience


Targeted at teenagers and avid gamers, Derelict is designed to provide an immersive survival experience on a mysterious island.


Getting Started


To embark on the Derelict adventure, follow these steps:
1. Clone the repository:
git clone https://github.com/RiddhimanRagurman/Derelict.git
2. Open the project in Unity.
3. Explore the island, complete tasks, and enjoy the survival adventure!
